Product overview

The goal is clarity. SmartFish Monitor focuses on live measurements, simple summaries, and a reliable sense of progress against your quota. No heavy menus, no complex flows, only the essentials.

What SmartFish Monitor does

Using ultrasonic sensors, the system measures how much fish is in the capture tank and emails the customer the weight. Simple setup, simple view, clear numbers.

You see current levels, recent change, and totals for the period you care about. The interface avoids clutter, it keeps the focus on values that help you act.

How it works, three steps

1
Measure displacement of water in the capture tank, ultrasonic sensor reads level change.
2
Calculate fish weight from level change, tank area and water density are used for conversion.
3
Send information to customers, email notifications include current totals and quota status.

Accuracy depends on calibration. Enter the tank geometry and the correct water density for your conditions, then verify with a known load.

Quotas and alerts

Customers can set quotas for day, month, year, kilograms. A quota is the catch weight limit you choose or that is defined by regulators. Warnings are sent as emails and LED when 75 percent of your quota is met. When the full quota is met, an email is sent.

Email is the primary channel for notices. The LED on the device gives a quick local hint about your state.

Security and privacy

Local storage for configuration, emails sent via Google with encryption in transit. No customer information is collected at this time.

Configuration stays on the device or on the local controller. Reports are delivered by email, which keeps the footprint small.

Depth resolution is 0.002 m. Weight conversion depends on calibration of tank area and water density.

What alerts do I get

Email and LED when 75 percent of quota is reached, email when the quota is fully reached.

Can I use it on battery only

Yes, the controller runs on battery power for the demo configuration.

Who is it for

What is the implementation like

An email is sent, next actions depend on your process and rules.
